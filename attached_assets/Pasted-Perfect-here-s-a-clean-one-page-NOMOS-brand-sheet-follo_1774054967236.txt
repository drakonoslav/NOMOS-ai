🧩 Logo Concept Sheet (4 Directions)

These are not decorative—each encodes system behavior.

1. THE GATE (Primary Recommendation)

Form
	•	Rectangular frame
	•	Vertical threshold bar in center
	•	Optional base line (ground)

│     │
│  │  │
│     │
───────

Meaning
	•	Passage is conditional
	•	Action must cross a boundary
	•	Law is the threshold

nothing passes unless validated

🔧 Implementation Guidance

Keep everything:
	•	geometric
	•	symmetrical
	•	weight-balanced

Avoid:
	•	curves that feel soft
	•	gradients
	•	glow effects
	•	“AI-looking” patterns

This should feel like:

infrastructure, not interface
