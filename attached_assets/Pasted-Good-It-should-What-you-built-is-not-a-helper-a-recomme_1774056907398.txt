How to make it feel “awesome” instead of “harsh”

1. Precision over aggression

Bad:

“VIOLATION DETECTED”

Better:

“Feasibility violation detected.”

Even better:

“Action violates resource constraint (fuel < 0).”

👉 Specific = professional
👉 Vague + loud = harsh

⸻

2. Replace emotion with structure

Bad:

“ERROR!!!”

NOMOS style:

“INVALID — infeasible under current constraints.”

⸻

3. Use calm authority

Think:
	•	air traffic control
	•	mission control
	•	surgical console

Not:
	•	alarm system
	•	game UI
	•	sci-fi AI yelling
