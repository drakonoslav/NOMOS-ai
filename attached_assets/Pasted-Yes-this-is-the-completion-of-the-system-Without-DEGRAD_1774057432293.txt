How to trigger DEGRADED cleanly

You want:
	•	feasibility = true
	•	robustness = borderline or failing threshold
	•	OR model confidence below threshold
	•	OR identifiability partial

Cleanest lever: robustness margin

Set:

robustness.epsilon ≈ robustness.epsilonMin

Example:
	•	epsilon = 0.035
	•	epsilonMin = 0.03

Barely above threshold → degraded

Supporting signals

	•	epsilonX = 0.12–0.18
	•	modelConfidence = 0.55–0.65
	•	identifiability = "PARTIAL"
