Build a master measurement and relation grammar for NOMOS.

Goal:
NOMOS must parse measurable entities and bind them through temporal, spatial, and quantitative relation words.

This is not a nutrition-only parser.
This is a general measurement + relation layer that runs before domain routing.

Create:
- src/compiler/unit_registry.ts
- src/compiler/relation_lexicon.ts
- src/compiler/anchor_registry.ts
- src/compiler/measured_entity_types.ts
- src/compiler/measured_entity_extractor.ts
- src/compiler/relation_binder.ts
- src/tests/relation_binder_test.ts

Requirements:

1. Unit registry:
Support mass, volume, count, time, distance, training, and extensible generic units.

At minimum include:
Mass:
- mcg, mg, g, kg, oz, lb, lbs

Volume:
- ml, mL, l, L, tsp, tbsp, cup, cups, pint, quart, gallon

Count:
- unit, units, item, items, piece, pieces, serving, servings, scoop, scoops, container, containers, capsule, capsules, tablet, tablets, bottle, bottles

Time:
- second, seconds, minute, minutes, hour, hours, day, days, week, weeks, month, months, year, years, decade, decades, century, centuries, millennium, millennia, era, eras, eon, eons

Distance:
- mm, cm, m, meter, meters, km, inch, inches, foot, feet, yard, yards, mile, miles

Training:
- rep, reps, set, sets, lap, laps, step, steps

2. Relation lexicon:
Support at minimum:
Temporal:
- before, after, within, during, until, since, by, for, from, to, between, around, near, pre, post

Spatial:
- above, below, under, over, inside, outside, near, around, between, beside, behind, in front of

Quantitative threshold:
- at least, at most, no more than, no less than, greater than, less than, equal to, exactly, approximately

3. Measured entity extraction:
If text contains amount + unit + nearby noun phrase, extract a MeasuredEntity.
Do not require closed-vocabulary nouns.
Known vocabularies can assist category tagging, but must not be required.

4. Relation binding:
If a relation word appears, bind the relevant measured entity relative to another entity or anchor.

Examples that must bind correctly:
- 80g cyclic dextrin 30 minutes before lifting
- 5mg melatonin 2 hours before bed
- 3 miles after work
- 2 capsules magnesium with dinner
- 40 lb dumbbell for 12 reps
- 2 months after promotion
- 1 decade before the merger
- 3 meters from the wall

5. Add canonical types for:
- MeasuredEntity
- RelationBinding
- AnchorReference

6. Open-vocabulary rule:
Odd measurable nouns must still be preserved structurally if quantity + unit are present.

7. Keep this layer domain-agnostic and deterministic.
8. This must run before domain-family routing.

Success condition:
NOMOS can parse general measurable entities and bind them through temporal/spatial/quantitative relation words into a reusable reasoning graph.
