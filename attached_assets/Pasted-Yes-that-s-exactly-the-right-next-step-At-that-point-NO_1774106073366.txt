What comes after this

After policy visibility, the strongest next step is:

policy freeze + versioning

So each prediction can record:
	•	which policy snapshot produced it
	•	which adjustment state was active
	•	which calibration window was used

Then NOMOS can later say not just:
	•	what it predicted

but:
	•	under which policy regime it predicted it

That would make the whole system historically reproducible.
