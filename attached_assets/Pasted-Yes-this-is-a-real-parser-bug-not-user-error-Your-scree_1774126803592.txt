	•	UNCERTAINTIES:

You clearly provided:
	•	A: Make oats, whey, and yogurt bowl.
	•	B: Make eggs with oats and banana.

So the candidate detector is either:
	•	receiving the wrong extracted block, or
	•	using a brittle pattern that misses valid A: / B: lines after bad section splitting.

But your app prompt template itself shows:

Bottom line

My recommendation on the fix itself

The parser should normalize headings and candidate formats up front.

Good parsing rule

Accept headings case-insensitively:
	•	STATE
	•	CONSTRAINTS
	•	UNCERTAINTIES
	•	CANDIDATES
	•	OBJECTIVE

with optional trailing colon.

Then capture content until the next recognized heading.

Good candidate rule

Accept candidate starts like:
	•	A:
	•	A.
	•	A)
	•	B:
	•	B.
	•	B)

That removes needless brittleness.
