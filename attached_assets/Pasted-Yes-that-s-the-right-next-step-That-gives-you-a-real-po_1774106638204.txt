Why this matters

Single-run replay tells you:
	•	how policies differ on one case

The bench tells you:
	•	how policies behave systematically

That’s the difference between:
	•	curiosity
and
	•	evidence

What this should eventually let NOMOS say

Examples:
	•	“Policy v3 achieved the highest exact-match rate across 42 nutrition runs.”
	•	“Policy v2 reduced over-aggressive forecasts but increased unresolved outcomes.”
	•	“Policy v1 was strongest on direction-match but weakest on confidence calibration.”
	•	“Policy v3 appears most conservative under shallow-history conditions.”

That’s the right level of judgment.

The key discipline

Do not let the bench change policy automatically yet.

First:
	•	replay
	•	measure
	•	compare
	•	summarize

Only after that should you consider assisted promotion.
